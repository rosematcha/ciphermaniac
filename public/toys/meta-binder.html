Meta Binder

Build a personal binder playset based on real event usage. Pick the events and archetypes you care about, then see every card laid out in 4x3 binder pages with the highest count played anywhere in the meta.

Cards are only shown if they appeared in at least four decks in a single event. Counts reflect the maximum copies played in any included list.

Display Options

0% — show all

Cards must clear the threshold both meta-wide and within the archetype. A card in 80% of one archetype but only 1% of all decks will still be hidden from that archetype's section.

0% — always show max

Sets how popular a copy count must be before it's suggested. At 0% the highest count seen in any deck is used. Raise this to ignore outlier counts — e.g. at 10%, a count only one deck runs won't inflate the suggestion.
